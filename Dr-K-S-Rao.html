Dr. K S Rao

Executive Director – AAFM India

K S Rao is a seasoned financial leader with 30+ years of experience in the mutual fund industry, specializing in sales, distribution, marketing, strategic planning, and investor education. As Executive Vice-President at Aditya Birla Sun Life AMC Ltd., he has been instrumental in spearheading financial literacy initiatives that have impacted millions across India.

Key Expertise:

  • Investor Education & Financial Literacy – Conceptualized Nivesh Mahakumbh, ForHER, Fauji Nivesh, My First Pay Cheque, and Money Lab, among others.
  • Strategic Leadership – Driving industry-leading programs that set benchmarks for investor awareness.
  • Mutual Fund & Portfolio Management – Deep expertise across the mutual fund value chain.
